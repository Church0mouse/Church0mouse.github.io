Edmund Mcmillen

An image of game developer and artist Edmund McMillen

Edmund Charles McMillen (born March 2, 1980) is an American video game designer and artist known for his Flash game visual style.

His most notable works include 2010's side-scroller Super Meat Boy and 2011's roguelike game The Binding of Isaac and its remake.
